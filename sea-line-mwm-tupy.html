MWM, a Tupy company, a traditional diesel engine manufacturer present in Brazil since 1953, is presenting its various solutions dedicated to the nautical market, for leisure and work boats.
 

MWM has modern 4.8L and 7.2L engines in 4- and 6-cylinder in-line configurations, which are equipped with closed cooling systems, turbocharged and electronically controlled.

The complete solution includes options of angular, parallel or V-Drive transmission, the availability of Konrad sterndrive, and modern control system including joysticks.
In partnership with Konrad, a company renowned worldwide for the development and production of propellers for high-performance boats, MWM is complementing its propulsion portfolio to serve a highly relevant market. These are high-quality, durable products with excellent operational efficiency.

MWM, appointed by MAN as the master distributor of engines and spare parts for the marine and off-highway segment in Brazil, complements its portfolio of propulsion solutions with the MAN I6, V8 and V12 marine engines. MAN engines for marine applications are the best options on the market for leisure and work boats.

Compact and with excellent power density, they guarantee high performance from low revs, without sacrificing efficient fuel consumption.

Like the MWM engine range, MAN engines can be delivered ready for installation with transmission, panels and cab controls, available in Brazil through MWM and its accredited network for MAN engines, offering engines, spare parts and technical assistance throughout the country.

Generator sets from 5kW to 38kW: Northern Lights, a company synonymous of quality, robustness and technology in the global marine segment, is now present in Brazil, represented by the strength of MWM and its network of parts and services. MWM/Northern Lights generator sets are supplied in a power range from 5 and 38 kW.

Generating sets from 100kW to 290kW: the Series 12 range of 4.8 and 7.2 liter marine engines equip MWM marine generating sets. The quality, technology and reliability of the renowned MWM generator sets have incorporated their products into the marine segment, with a high level of national content, which means lower maintenance costs and great availability of spare parts. They also have the lowest noise level on the domestic market.

In partnership with Swedish engine manufacturer OXE Marine, it brings the first diesel outboard engines to the market.

With a power range of from 150 and 300 hp compared to conventional gasoline outboards, OXE engines offer significantly more torque, reduced greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions, better fuel consumption, far superior reliability and lower operating costs.

In addition to power transmission solutions, MWM, in partnership with Deep Sea Electronics, manufacturers of the brand's current generator set controllers, supply controllers and screens specifically for the marine segment.
These two products combine the renowned quality of Deep Sea Electronics products with the functionality and elegance required for work and leisure boats.

All with CAN J1939 connection and IP67 protection against ingress of dirt and water:
 
  • Individual controller for operating and monitoring the thrusters, with a state-of-the-art 7" color screen, operated by buttons and touch screen, with automatic brightness adjustment, Ethernet port, USB port, two inputs for video cameras;
     
  • An excellent choice in terms of functionality and robustness for monitoring and controlling protection systems with a state-of-the-art 12" screen, full touch screen and external operating buttons, 1GHz processor speed and 16GB flash storage capacity, WiFi connection for monitoring and diagnostics, GPS input, provision for future Bluetooth application, USB port, input for two video cameras and remote monitoring.

It is important to note that MWM's portfolio of products for the marine segment also includes throttle controls, ignition switches - with integrated emergency system, modern displays, in other words, the complete and integrated solution for the marine segment.

In addition to the engines and generator sets dedicated to the nautical segment, MWM is exhibiting its consolidated and renowned range of parts, which include:

Genuine MWM: developed according to the technical specifications of MWM engines, tested and approved by the company's engineering department, respecting the strictest quality standards with ISO 9001 certification and in compliance with environmental laws.

MWM Optionals: approved by MWM engineering and quality, it brings new parts options with a focus on increasing competitiveness for MWM products.

Master Parts®: were developed with the aim of expanding the spare parts portfolio, as well as serving and marketing multi-brand products. And it has more than 1,400 items available.

In addition to the traditional parts lines, MWM now also has a line of MAN Engines parts for the marine and off-road segments, which are now part of the spare parts portfolio and will be available to consumers at specialized distribution points in the MWM/MAN network.
